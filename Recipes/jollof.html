Jollof

The best Naija Jollof

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Rice
  • Chilli pepper
  • Red onion
  • Bayleaf

Steps

  1. Ingredients!
  2. blending together the roughly chopped fresh tomatoes, red bell pepper, scotch bonnet and red onion in a blender until a smooth pepper and tomato sauce /puree.
  3. In a lidded large pan, fry the finely chopped red onion over medium heat until softened. Stir in the tomato paste and garlic (if using) and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the blended sauce and stir together with bay leaves, all-purpose seasoning, dried thyme and extra salt to taste.
  5. Bring sauce to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to simmer for 15 minutes to allow it to thicken the colour will become a deeper red too.
